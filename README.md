# Phishing Email Analysis

## Objective

The objective of this task was to analyze a phishing email sample and identify common phishing characteristics used by attackers to deceive users.

## Tools Used

* Web Browser
* Sample Phishing Email Screenshot
* Microsoft Word
* PDF Converter

## Phishing Indicators Identified

### 1. Suspicious Sender Address

The sender email address used a domain that appeared different from the official PayPal domain, indicating possible email spoofing.

### 2. Urgent Language

The email claimed that the account would be disabled within 24 hours, creating pressure on the recipient to act immediately.

### 3. Generic Greeting

The message addressed the recipient as "Dear PayPal customer" rather than using a specific name.

### 4. Request for Sensitive Information

The email requested account verification and personal information, a common phishing tactic.

### 5. Suspicious Link

The email contained a button directing the user to confirm account information. Such links are often used to redirect users to fraudulent websites.

### 6. Grammar and Formatting Issues

The email contained awkward wording and punctuation errors that are often seen in phishing campaigns.

## Conclusion

Based on the identified indicators, the email is highly likely to be a phishing attempt. Users should avoid clicking links, downloading attachments, or sharing sensitive information when receiving similar emails.
